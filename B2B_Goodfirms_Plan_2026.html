This is the working plan for Orient's GoodFirms Pro+ subscription. GoodFirms is a B2B software vendor directory — companies looking to hire a software development partner search on GoodFirms and select from ranked listings. Orient currently has a free profile that is outdated and miscategorised. This plan upgrades to Pro+ to gain visibility in Australia and the UK, and to support the AI Native campaign by positioning spec-driven development in the profile description.
Refund / Cancellation Policy — Confirmed: No Refund GoodFirms does not offer refunds. If Orient downgrades or cancels, it will only take effect at the end of the 12-month subscription — not immediately. There is no early exit. This has been confirmed with the GoodFirms account manager. Marketing manager approval is required before proceeding with payment.
4 Things to Know
Summary for Approval
01
The proposal is to pay $2,000/year for a Pro+ listing
That is $167/month. The plan runs for 12 months as a pilot. After month 3 (August), the Digital Lead will compile lead and citation data and present an investment decision — scale up, maintain, downgrade to Pro ($1,500/year), or stop. No automatic renewal without review.
02
Pro+ breaks Top 10 in Australia — Pro does not
Australia is Orient's primary ICP market outside Vietnam. With Pro, Orient ranks #15 in Australia — effectively invisible. With Pro+, Orient ranks #8, entering the Top 10 that most buyers browse. The $500 difference between Pro and Pro+ is the cost of that visibility gap. The UK is a secondary market — Pro+ also improves UK ranking.
03
GoodFirms does not have listings for LP02 or LP03
Searched all available GoodFirms categories. There is no dedicated listing for code audit services (LP02) or legacy system modernisation (LP03). For LP01, the Vibe Coding Companies listing has been successfully negotiated into the Pro+ deal at rank #3, replacing the Singapore slot. This is a strong strategic fit — Orient's spec-driven approach is the direct alternative to vibe coding, so appearing at #3 creates clear contrast in the buyer's mind. For LP02 and LP03, the only lever remains the profile description.
04
5 leads/month is the directional target — not a guarantee
GoodFirms lead volume depends on ranking, profile quality, and review count. 5 leads/month is a benchmark to evaluate against at Q1 appraisal. The more immediate goal for months 1–2 is to collect baseline data, secure 3 client reviews, and confirm that the profile description accurately represents Orient's services including spec-driven development.
