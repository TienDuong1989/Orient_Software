02
Strategic Choices
Three deliberate decisions that shape how this system is built and run.
①
Track behavior before form submit - not just leads
High-intent visitors often leave before submitting a form - still researching, still evaluating. Without behavioral tracking, they disappear unidentified. This system captures their intent signals and brings them back through retarget campaigns across any ad channel.
Why this matters
When a visitor later submits a form, we know exactly which intent group they belong to - what pages they visited, how deep they engaged, and which campaign brought them back. This makes retarget optimization possible, not just trackable.
How it works
13 events via GTM + dev. Score 1-85 before submit. Audiences built from real behavioral signals - starting with LinkedIn, reusable across Google, Email, and other ad channels in Q3+.
②
Organic first - Paid activates only after audiences are validated
Apr-Jun organic only. LinkedIn Paid activates Jul - after audiences reach 300+ members. Paid amplifies what works, not what we hope will work.
Why this matters
Running paid ads to unvalidated audiences wastes budget. Organic traffic in Apr-Jun builds real intent signals - Paid in Jul retargets only proven-intent users.
How it works
Gate 2: ≥300 members per segment before Paid activates. Every LinkedIn ad impression in Jul goes to a user who has already shown real intent on the website.
③
LinkedIn now - event architecture built for multi-channel expansion
Event tracking built once - feeds all channels simultaneously. Adding a new channel is configuration, not development.
Why this matters
One event fires once and flows into GA4, LinkedIn, Google Ads, and the scoring system at the same time. No duplicate setup. No inconsistent data between channels. No dev work when a new channel is added.
How it works
13 GTM events fire regardless of channel. Audience data stored in system. Q3+ expansion is configuration - not a rebuild.
