01 — Overview
Overview
A 4-month link building plan running alongside the AI Native content campaign (May–August 2026).
Goal

Build Orient's authority as the defining source on spec-driven development — recognized by both Google and AI assistants.

1
Why This Strategy Exists
Orient is defining a new category — spec-driven development. Link building creates the foundation for Orient's blog articles to be recognized and cited by AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) as an authoritative source on this topic. The primary goal is AI citation and brand authority, not direct organic traffic.
2
Why Links Point to Blog Articles, Not Landing Pages
The 3 landing pages (Greenfield, Brownfield, Legacy) are conversion pages — not discovery pages. Links point to blog articles in the funnel. Those articles rank, earn traffic, and pass link equity to the LPs through internal links. This keeps LP content clean for conversion while the blog ecosystem does the ranking work.
02 — Tactics
3 Link Building Tactics
All tactics run in parallel. Total commitment is 7–9 links/month from Link Insertion and Guest Post combined. Link Exchange is best effort — no committed number.
Link Insertion
Niche Edit · Primary
Insert a link into an existing, already-indexed article on a third-party site. Highest ROI tactic — the host article already has authority and traffic. Link has value immediately. Self-outreach combined with freelancer.
Guest Post
Paid Placement · Primary
Publish articles on third-party sites with a link back to Orient. Self-outreach combined with freelancer support. Prioritize sites with real traffic, editorial standards, and AI-related content.
Link Exchange
Direct A ↔ B · Best Effort
Direct reciprocal exchange — Orient links to a partner site, partner links back to Orient. Self-outreach via email. Building network from scratch. DR 40+ partners only.

04 — Target Articles
Primary & Optional Targets
All articles only receive links 2 weeks after their publish date. No exceptions. Links are distributed flexibly across primary targets each month — no hard per-article quota. what is spec driven development always receives the highest link priority.
Internal Linking In addition to receiving external backlinks, all target articles should also be supported by internal links from other relevant pages on the site to maximize link equity flow into each target. An internal linking checklist will be updated in June.
Why Only One Service Page This strategy is focused on the AI Native campaign. Link building budget is concentrated on blog articles for traffic and GEO, rather than spread across multiple service pages. software development services is the only service page targeted because it carries the highest volume and is most directly tied to the campaign — adding more service pages would dilute the impact per page.
