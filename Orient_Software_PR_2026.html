What PR Newswire Does
PR Newswire distributes the press release to a network of media outlets that have subscribed to their feed. These outlets automatically publish the release — this is called syndication. The KPI link count measures how many of these auto-pickups occur. Most syndicated outlets are general news sites. Tech-specific coverage (earned media) happens when a tech journalist independently decides to cover the story — this cannot be bought or guaranteed.

📌 Important — KPI & Syndication
KPI link counts = syndication only. When a release is submitted, media outlets that have subscribed to PR Newswire automatically pick up and publish the release — these auto-pickups generate the KPI link count. The majority of these outlets are general news sites that subscribe to all industries, not tech-specific media.
Tech-specific coverage = earned media. Tech outlets (e.g. iTnews, ZDNet, TechRadar) may independently choose to edit and republish the release as their own article — this is earned media and cannot be predicted or guaranteed. However, this is where the most relevant tech audience coverage comes from.
⚠ KPI figures are for reference only and are not included in the formal contract. PR Newswire provides KPI estimates in proposals (pitching stage) but does not legally commit to specific link numbers. Actual results depend on editorial decisions by each outlet.

Note for Content Team — Press Release Requirements
Format Required
PR Newswire requires a news announcement format — not thought leadership or opinion. Twist only the headline and first paragraph into announcement format. The intellectual content inside remains unchanged.
✅ Example: "Orient Software releases whitepaper on why legacy modernisation fails"
Length
400–600 words recommended. APAC English Only is flat fee (no extra charge). Australia and Europe English Only charge per 100 words above 400 — target 500w to control cost.
Headline
Under 110 characters · 10–15 words recommended. Must lead with the news hook, not the company name.
Category at Submit
Select Technology / IT — ensures release is prioritised to tech wire outlets.
Leadership Quote
One quote only. State the insight directly, then attribute it. Do not use: "We are excited/delighted to announce" — write for a CTO who will close the tab if it sounds like a pitch.
Do NOT Include
Pricing · 2027 internal targets · Case studies not yet formally published · Hyperlinks: limit to 2–3 links max.
